How ReWear Works

1

List Your Items

Upload photos and details of clothing you no longer wear. Set your points value or choose direct swap.

2

Browse & Connect

Discover items from other users. Send swap requests with points or offer your own items in exchange.

3

Swap & Share

Agree on terms, meet up or ship items, and complete your sustainable fashion exchange.

Why Choose ReWear?

Eco-Friendly

Reduce textile waste and your carbon footprint by giving clothes a second life.

Cost-Effective

Get new-to-you clothing without spending money. Use points or direct swaps.

Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who care about sustainable fashion.

Safe & Secure

Verified users, secure transactions, and community ratings ensure safe exchanges.
